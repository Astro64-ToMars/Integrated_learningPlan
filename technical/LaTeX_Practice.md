## Why LaTeX?
- LaTeX: Document markup language used to create professional-looking documents with consistent formatting
- Useful for typesettign mathematical notation to be more easily readable
- Produces PDF files that can be saved and opened on any device
- Free, Open-source, Highly-customizable

**Fun fact:** LaTeX is not pronounced "Lay-tex", it is pronounced "Lay-tek" because the "X" at the end is actually a special greek letter.
